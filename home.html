Tandra is a state of mind experienced in meditation, the state of higher consciousness between being asleep and awake. This mental state is also known as a state of "yogic drowsiness." Tandra is the first one of the four main states of mind that a yogi must pass through before reaching the final state - nirvikalpa samadhi, or the non-mind. In meditation, tandra is followed by yoga nidra, or the yogic sleep.
